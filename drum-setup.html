Audio recording
...

This patches two audio channels between the Roland drum brain and Bitwig DAW for recording audio tracks on the laptop. The brain is also the master monitor and has down mixed mono out to the Behringer interface which is connected to speakers and headphones.

  1. Use drum brain USB mode VENDOR with
  2. Bitwig driver model ASIO,
  3. select the drum brain, the Roland TD-17 device (only available when midi disabled in brain)

The down mixed mono monitor is fine, but recording should occur only in Bitwig where both channels are present.

Midi recording
...

This sends midi from the brain to a Bitwig instrument track via USB, and monitors the master on a low latency interface connected to the headphones and speakers.

  1. Use drum brain USB mode GENERIC with
  2. Bitwig driver model ASIO,
  3. Selecting the Behringer UMC device.
